Briquettes from any Type of Wood

Sawdust briquettes can be made from all types of wood, as long as the sawdust is dry and finely ground. The process involves compressing the sawdust into compact, dense briquettes, which makes them easy to handle and burn efficiently.

The briquetting process is quick and involves minimal use of binders or chemicals, allowing for a high-quality product. These briquettes burn longer and cleaner than loose sawdust, offering an eco-friendly fuel alternative with a simple, fast production method.
